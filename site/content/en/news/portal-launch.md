The **Pathogen Portal CZ** is an open-access platform for pathogen surveillance data in the Czech Republic. 
The portal brings together data from clinical, genomic, and environmental surveillance streams in one place.

Initial dashboards cover SARS-CoV-2, influenza, and wastewater-based epidemiology.
